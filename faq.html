Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are the flowers really handmade?

Yes! Every flower is crocheted by Sarah Lamb using high-quality yarn, wire stems, and lots of love.

2. How long do the flowers last?

Forever! Unlike real flowers, our blooms never wilt and require no watering or sunlight.

3. Can I request custom colors or arrangements?

Yes, please use the Contact page to send us a message. We’ll discuss your request personally!

4. Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within the U.S., but international shipping may be added soon.

5. What are your flowers made from?

We use a mix of acrylic and cotton yarn, floral wire, and sometimes felt for extra detail.

6. Can I send this as a gift?

Absolutely! Let us know if you’d like us to include a note or gift wrap.

7. How should I care for my crochet flowers?

Keep out of direct sunlight to prevent fading. Dust gently with a dry cloth or can of air.

8. Can I return or exchange my order?

Due to the handmade nature, all sales are final unless your order arrives damaged.

9. How long does it take to ship?

Orders are made and shipped within 3-5 business days unless otherwise noted.

10. Where are you based?

We’re based in Arkansas, where all items are handmade and shipped with care.<
