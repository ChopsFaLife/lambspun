About Lambspun Florals

Every petal tells a story. Handcrafted by Sarah Lamb in Arkansas, Lambspun Florals delivers timeless crochet arrangements made with care, tradition, and heart.

Meet the Artist

Sarah Lamb has been crocheting since childhood. Born in Australia and raised in Canada, she now calls Arkansas home. Her unique blend of pattern design and floral imagination brought Lambspun Florals to life. Each piece is created with vintage charm, modern elegance, and a love for enduring beauty.
