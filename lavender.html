From the Artist

I’ve always adored the calming beauty of lavender — its fragrance, its softness, its timeless elegance. I created this crochet lavender bundle as a quiet, everlasting bloom for those moments of peace in your space. Each stem is crafted with care to capture the natural charm of the real flower, without the worry of wilting.

Care Instructions

  • Do not wash or submerge in water.
  • Dust gently with a dry cloth or canned air.
  • Keep out of direct sunlight to preserve color vibrancy.
